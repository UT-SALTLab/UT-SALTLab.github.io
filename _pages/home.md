Our research group investigates machine learning models and methodologies for spoken language and audio processing. We are particularly interested in multi-modal learning (for example, audio-visual), as well as approaches based on unsupervised/self-supervised learning for low-resource languages and domains. See our [Research](research) page for more details.

We are part of the [Computer Science department](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/) at [The University of Texas at Austin](https://www.utexas.edu/), and are located near the heart of campus in the beautiful and bright [Gates-Dell Complex](https://www.architectmagazine.com/project-gallery/bill-melinda-gates-computer-science-complex-and-dell-computer-science-hall). 

## Joining the Lab

 We are always on the lookout for motivated students interested in joining the lab!

**Current UT students (Ph.D., M.S., and undergraduate)** interested in joining our lab should email David Harwath directly. Please include some information about yourself, including a copy of your CV/resume and a description of your background relevant to our research.

**Prospective graduate students** should apply directly to the Computer Science department's graduate program. You can find more information [here](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/faq/graduate).
